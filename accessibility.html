Pattern 4.2.6: Make the Relationship Clear Between Controls and the Content They Affect

Overview

The visual association between {{WHAT1}} and {{WHAT2}} isn't sufficiently clear.

Details

The relationship between controls and related content should be completely clear and unambiguous. However, {{WHAT1}} {{WHAT2}}

Impact

In most cases, the user of a screen magnifier only sees a small part of the viewport at a time. As the screen magnifier moves horizontally accross the viewport the user may have difficulty associating {{WHAT1}} and {{WHAT2}}.

Recommendation

Place {{WHAT1}} and {{WHAT2}} inside a section, and have clear borders around the section.

Source

Pattern 4.2.6: Make the Relationship Clear Between Controls and the Content They Affect from the Making Content Usable for People with Cognitive and Learning Disabilities Working Group Note.
